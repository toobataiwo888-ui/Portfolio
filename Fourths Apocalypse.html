FOURTH’S APOCALYPSE

The world has ended three times. Now it begins again.

THE WORLD: ARTH

The planet Arth is no stranger to catastrophe.

Over countless eons, three asteroid impacts reshaped its destiny—each one marking a change of an age, not its end.

First Asteroid – The Nuclear Spark
A neighboring celestial body collided with a young Arth, igniting a Nuclear Spark deep within its forming core. This impact not only gave birth to the moon, but embedded volatile alien energy in the planet’s heart. These buried fragments still pulse with power, silently shaping evolution.

Second Asteroid – The Age-Ender
This asteroid struck millions of years later, eradicating the dominant species—the dinosaurs—and ushering in the age of mammals. But evolution didn’t reset. It adapted.

Third Asteroid – The Propagator
A lesser-known strike, buried in forgotten myth. The Propagator introduced an alien microbial spark to an early tribe, accelerating their mental and physical development. Humanity was born—not from random evolution, but from cosmic intervention. And it was this alteration that made humans the only species capable of surviving the apocalypse to come.

Fourth Asteroid – The Catalyst
It was predicted to miss.
It didn’t.

The fourth asteroid slammed into the country of Sur, triggering a global shockwave. But this wasn’t just a meteor—it was a dimensional vessel, carrying unknown microorganisms and exposing the planet to radiation unlike anything ever recorded. Compounding this event, a secretive biohazard facility in Sur collapsed, releasing experimental pathogens that fused with the alien microbes.

This dual apocalypse—a Dimensional Invasion and Biohazard Cataclysm—twisted Arth’s ecosystem forever:

Wildlife mutated into savage beasts.
Entire landscapes evolved into alien terrains.
The very rules of biology fractured.

85% of humanity perished.

But something… awakened.

---

THE PLAYER CHARACTERS: SAM (Code Names: Fourth & Fifth)

You play as Sam—either Samuel (Fourth) or Samantha (Fifth).

A human, born and raised in the pre-apocalyptic world. Sam was a special forces soldier from Sur, trained in every form of modern and ancient combat. But after witnessing the horrific truth behind Operation Genesis—a covert military program that violated every law of nature and ethics—Sam deserted, becoming a global fugitive.

Sam wandered the world, studying martial arts and surviving in the shadows, seeking meaning. Then the Fourth Asteroid struck—and changed everything.

Now, Sam’s mission is to:

• Defeat mutated monsters and dimensional threats
• Rebuild fractured communities
• Unlock the secrets of evolution and the Fourth Asteroid
• Prepare the world for what’s next

Sam is no ordinary survivor. They are a Conscious—a human being capable of absorbing Evolution Stones dropped by monsters and transforming them into biological power.

Through this, Sam gains abilities, mutations, and enhanced stats—an edge in a world that has outgrown the rules of man.

---

WORLDLINE LAW: ONLY ONE CAN EXIST

Fourth and Fifth cannot exist in the same worldline or timeline.

They are quantum opposites—mirrors that fracture the universe when facing each other. The presence of both would unravel reality.

Thus, only one can live in a single world.

Choose Samuel (Fourth) or Samantha (Fifth).

Your story diverges depending on who survives.
The other’s existence becomes a mystery… or a threat.

---

THE FINAL BOSS: GENERAL NATHAN – THE GENERAL

He was once Sam’s commanding officer.
A war hero. A mentor. A killer.

Now, he is General Nathan, also known as The General—the final product of Operation Genesis.

During the Fourth Impact, The General was exposed to the highest concentration of dimensional energy. Unlike others, he didn’t die or mutate—he evolved beyond humanity.

Ability: Umbrakinesis – He manipulates pure darkness and dimensional energy.

His presence inflicts status debuffs: slowness, weakness, confusion, silence, and despair.
He commands the most powerful evolved monsters.
His will corrupts the land around him.

Defeating The General is not the end.
It is the unlocking of a deeper truth—and a new threat.

---

GAME SYSTEMS & PROGRESSION

Start at Level 1 with stats capped at 100 per attribute (Strength, Agility, Intelligence, Dexterity, etc.).

Evolution Stones: Dropped by mutated monsters. Absorb them to:
• Gain abilities (e.g. kinetic strikes, elemental limbs, bio-enhancements)
• Level up your stats
• Unlock passive traits (e.g. heightened perception, adaptive healing)

Mutation Stones: Rare drops allowing:
• Stat reshuffling
• Tactical mutations
• Temporary monster transformations

Combat Arsenal:
• Begin with hand-to-hand combat
• Raid bases to acquire firearms
• Complete quests to obtain high-grade swords, relic-tech, and armor

---

THE MONSTERS

Every region of Arth is overrun by grotesque lifeforms:

• Mutabeasts – Radiation-fused predators
• Voidhorrors – Creatures born from other dimensions
• Spliced Titans – Human-animal-machine hybrids from Operation Genesis gone wrong
• Elder Evolutionaries – Ancient organisms mutated into apex forms

They are not just enemies.
They are the future Arth might become.
